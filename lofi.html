The Marvels
Client : Disney
Work ordered : Animation Loop and Illustration

Embark on an artistic odyssey of superheroic proportions as Lofi Studio proudly unveils its collaboration with Disney for the much-anticipated cinematic spectacle, "The Marvels." This alliance represents a fusion of animation and illustration, bringing fans an unprecedented opportunity to channel their inner hero through the stroke of a pen.

Step into the role of creator and join forces with the luminous Kamala Khan, also known as Ms. Marvel, a beacon of inspiration and a testament to the power of imagination. Lofi Studio has harnessed cutting-edge digital tools to construct a realm where the lines between the fans and the fantastical blur, inviting you to sketch, render, and animate your own visions alongside the mighty Kamala.
